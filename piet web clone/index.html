PLACEMENT NEWS

Placement Success at PIET: A New Milestone in Excellence Panipat Institute of Engineering and Technology (PIET) has once again demonstrated its commitment to academic and professional excellence with an outstanding placement season this year. The institute continues to solidify its reputation as a premier educational institution by achieving remarkable placement statistics. Record-Breaking Placements This year, PIET has seen an unprecedented number of students securing placements with top-tier companies. The placement drive witnessed participation from over 200 renowned companies, offering lucrative job offers across diverse sectors. Notably, several students have received multiple job offers, showcasing the high demand for PIET graduates in the job market. Top Recruiters Leading companies from various industries have recruited from PIET, including multinational corporations such as Infosys, Wipro, TCS, Capgemini, and Cognizant. Additionally, several startups and emerging companies have also shown a keen interest in PIET students, highlighting the institute's ability to produce versatile and innovative professionals. Impressive Salary Packages The highest salary package offered this year is an impressive INR 20 LPA (Lakhs Per Annum), with the average salary package rising to INR 5.5 LPA. These figures reflect the high level of skills and competencies that PIET students bring to the table, making them highly sought after by employers. Looking Ahead With each passing year, PIET aims to raise the bar higher, striving for even greater placement success. The institute is dedicated to building strong industry connections and continuously updating its curriculum to align with evolving industry needs. PIET remains steadfast in its mission to provide world-class education and create future leaders.

AWARDS & ACHIVEMENTS

Panipat Institute of Engineering and Technology (PIET) has consistently demonstrated excellence across various domains, garnering prestigious accolades that underscore its commitment to quality education and holistic development. Notably, PIET was awarded the "Clean Campus Award" in 2017, recognizing its dedication to maintaining a pristine and eco-friendly campus environment, fostering a healthy and sustainable atmosphere for learning and growth. Further highlighting its academic prowess, PIET was named the "Best Engineering College in NCR" in 2014, a testament to its superior infrastructure, distinguished faculty, and innovative teaching methodologies. This recognition established PIET as a leading institution in the National Capital Region, attracting aspiring engineers from across the country. Additionally, the institute's robust placement record earned it the title of "Best Institute for Excellent Placement" in 2016. This accolade reflects PIET's unwavering focus on ensuring its students secure promising career opportunities with top-tier companies. The college's dedicated placement cell, comprehensive training programs, and strong industry connections have consistently resulted in impressive placement statistics, making it a preferred choice for both students and employers. These achievements collectively highlight PIET's dedication to fostering academic excellence, environmental stewardship, and career readiness, solidifying its reputation as a premier educational institution.
